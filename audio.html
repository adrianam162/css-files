This audio project, “Studying at a Cafe but it’s Raining,” was created to capture a calm and immersive atmosphere through ambient sound design. By combining café background noise with rain audio, I explored how layered sound can create emotion, improve storytelling, and enhance digital experiences. This project helped me better understand audio editing, sound balancing, and environmental sound composition.
